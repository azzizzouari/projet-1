Ron Weasley

Ronald Bilius "Ron" Weasley (b. 1 March, 1980) was an English pure-blood wizard, the sixth and youngest son of Arthur and Molly Weasley (née Prewett).
He was also the younger brother of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, George, and the elder brother of Ginny.
Ron and his siblings lived at the The Burrow, on the outskirts of Ottery St Catchpole, Devon.
